What We’re Offering

Apogee leveller is offering the best solutions for its customers to meet their needs.


Laser Land Leveller

Laser Land Leveller is a machine that is used to level the land from its average elevation most precisely. This contains levelling equipment such as a transmitter, receiver, control box, scrapper. Apogee’s Laser Land leveller ensures a smooth and precise surface which results in increasing yields, even distribution of water for irrigation, lessening the time in the irrigation process, and also reducing fertilizers, chemicals, and fuel requirements.

Learn More

GNSS Land Leveller

Gnss land leveller provides good levelling quality and improved efficiency than other methods. High land levelling quality is important for the future of agriculture and also is primary and key for water-saving irrigation. Even though laser land leveller gives a smooth surface but due to problems such as weak environment adaptability, limited operating range, etc. laser operating machines obstruct the land levelling operation’s development. The GNSS contains the GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, etc that offer the all-weather elevation data.
