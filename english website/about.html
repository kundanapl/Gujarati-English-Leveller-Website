About Us

Company profile

Headquartered in Gujarat, Apogee Precision Lasers is one of the most respected companies in India's manufacturing sector. Apogee Leveller is a leading company working on the latest technologies in the field of agriculture. Since the traditional method of land levelling was very cumbersome and did not give precise results. So, it was important to develop the technologies that could start a new era in the agriculture industry. Apogee leveller was established at the beginning of 2010. This is a segment of Apogee Precision Lasers.

Starting from the manufacturing of scrappers for land levelling, we began to develop the sensors based on new technologies and made farmers aware of them.

Our aim is the development of the agro-industry, bringing new technologies and helping farmers in increasing their yields and reducing water losses. Apogee Leveller pledges to the quality with the International certification ISO 9000: 2008. The Company started with the vision of facilitating the Agriculture Industry and farmers with the modern techniques of farming and land preparation.

What We Do

We primarily manufacture the most advanced and sophisticated Levelling Equipment and help to automate the process of farming through equipment like auto steering rice planter. We develop laser and GNSS land levelling equipment such as receiver, transmitter, control box, scrapper which help to level the land precisely. These laser and GNSS techniques help farmers in their growth and save the water by its proper distribution all over the field.

At Apogee Leveller, our auto steering rice planter automates the process of planting the seedlings, which saves time and reduces fatigue. This can also be operated at night and make the job hassle-free.

The most important reason behind the development of these technologies is to introduce the agriculture sector with modern innovative technologies in India. We, as a manufacturer, are highly committed to premium quality products and timely delivery.
