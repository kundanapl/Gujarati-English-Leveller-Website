Laser Land Leveller

Suitable for 35-60 HP

APL 2014

Apogee’s APL 2014 Laser Land Levelling tool kit is ideal for land levelling, approved by the Indian Government. This includes a long-range (800 meters) rotatory laser transmitter. This transmitter has ±10 accuracy with ±5°levelling range. A rotatory laser receiver with glass protection and metal covering has 360° reception range and 200 mm reception length. A control box that has an ergonomic design and 10-18 DC operating voltage. These are suitable for 35-60 HP tractors. This Apogee’s laser land leveller helps in reducing the time in irrigation, in water management, and in increasing the crop yields.
